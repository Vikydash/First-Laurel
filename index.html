Nautical-inspired style with panoramic
sea views

Our nautically inspired hotel stands on the end of Princes Wharf, a welcoming
dock for cruise liners in Summer. Our rooms offer panoramic views, and our
outdoor lap pool has a unique underwater viewing window. Downtown’s
vibrant retail and dining scene is a 10-minute walk away, or enjoy an authentic
taste of Aotearoa at FISH Restaurant or Bellini.
